The Vision

South Flagler House celebrates a prime waterfront location with iconic limestone-clad towers stretching 28 stories into the sky, anchored by a single podium wrapped in stepped gardens and lush landscaping — a new landmark for West Palm Beach.

The Residences

Condominium residences enjoy gracious scale with high ceilings, abundant fenestration, premium interior finishes, and grand outdoor spaces — seamlessly aligning comfort with luxury. Direct views of the Intracoastal Waterway and expansive ocean vistas define each home.

The Legacy

For its uncompromising quality and commitment to excellence, its astounding beauty both inside and out, South Flagler House will become a legacy property to be enjoyed for generations. Designed to endure, crafted to delight — a true masterpiece of timeless character.

About Liz

A life rooted in
Palm Beach.

Liz Callahan brings over a decade of experience in home design and styling to every client relationship. Though she has recently returned to Palm Beach, the city has been woven into the fabric of her life from the very beginning. Her family has been an integral part of Palm Beach since the 1950s — her grandparents were the first couple to pass the Florida Bar Exam, and her grandmother was among the first female attorneys in the state. Beloved in the North End of the Island, they built a legacy that Liz carries forward with pride.

Growing up in New Hampshire, Liz was one of the top tennis players in the state during her teen years, a passion she continues to pursue today. She attended the University of Montana on a Leadership, Achievement, and Service Scholarship — drawn west by her love of the outdoors. She then spent five years in Los Angeles, where she earned a degree in Interior Design from the Interior Designers Institute in Newport Beach, and also pursued acting, achieving union status as a member of SAG-AFTRA.

Before committing fully to real estate, Liz served as Development Director at Opportunity Early Childhood Education and Family Center — the oldest charity in Palm Beach. Her community involvement deepened her knowledge of the island's neighborhoods and reinforced what she already knew: that Palm Beach was where she was meant to build her career. Within her very first week as an agent with Corcoran, she had a home go under contract.

With an outgoing personality and a network that spans Palm Beach to the Northeast, Liz is a force in the field. Her passion is unwavering — she will stop at nothing to ensure that buyers and sellers alike feel protected, fought for, and ultimately delighted with their decisions. Drawing from her interior design background, she is masterful at staging homes for sellers and interpreting aesthetic vision for buyers. When she is not working, you can find her with her rescue dog Mika, honing her golf game, or immersed in the arts.
