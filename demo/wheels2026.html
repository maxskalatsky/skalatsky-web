Our Story

It Started with a Group of Friends and One Big Idea.

Max Skalatsky started this ride in 2024 with a group of friends who came together to support Els for Autism through a meaningful cycling fundraiser. That first ride raised over $27,000 for Els for Autism and the life-changing work they do for individuals with autism and their families.

Inspired by the incredible impact and support, we've decided to do the ride again in 2026.

100% of donations raised through this ride will directly support Els for Autism and the life-changing services, programs, education, and resources they provide for individuals with autism and their families.

Why We Ride

Supporting Els for Autism

Join us for a meaningful morning of community, movement, and impact as we ride together in support of Els for Autism. This special fundraising ride brings cyclists of different levels together for a safe, inspiring, and purpose-driven experience while supporting the incredible programs and services provided by Els for Autism.

Community

Ride alongside cyclists of every level who share a common purpose — supporting individuals with autism and their families.

Movement

A safe, inspiring, and supported ride through beautiful Jupiter, Florida — pace groups for every ability level.

Impact

Every mile ridden helps create impact. Every dollar raised funds programs, education, and resources at Els for Autism.
