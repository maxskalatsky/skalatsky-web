The Model

Where institutional capital meets exceptional influence

Team One Hundred was built on a simple premise: the most influential people in sports and entertainment deserve the same access to institutional investment that elite family offices and sovereign wealth funds take for granted.

We source it. We structure it. We deliver it — with your legacy in mind from day one. No pooled fund. No financial advisor. No talent agency. Something the market has never had before.

01

Exclusive Access

We open doors to venture capital, private equity, and direct deal opportunities that are unavailable to most individuals — regardless of their net worth or fame. Our institutional network is the asset.

02

Elite Network

Membership positions you alongside founders, operators, and institutional investors who do not typically sit in the same room as athletes. That circle compounds in ways a return multiple cannot.

03

Philanthropic Alignment

Every deal we structure includes a giving component tied to causes you care about. Your career made you visible. This makes your impact last long after it ends.

04

Education & Guidance

We explain every deal in plain language and ensure the right people in your life are part of every decision. You stay in control. We handle everything else.
