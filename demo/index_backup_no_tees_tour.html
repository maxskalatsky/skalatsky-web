A New Kind

Your driver can't save you.

We focus on the short game, where champions are made.

No Tees Tour™ is a competitive short-game golf tour built at the intersection of sport and culture. Fast-paced. 1v1. Single elimination. No tee shots required.

Think March Madness meets a block party — on a Par-3 course. Every match is a bracket. Every bracket builds a champion. Every stop builds a movement.
