The Origin

Born of

Pioneers

Whiskey JYPSI didn't come from a boardroom. It came from a belief that the best bourbon should carry a story worth telling — made by people who refuse to follow the map everyone else is reading.

"We made something we're proud to pour. That's where it starts."

Co-created by Grammy Award-winning country music artist Eric Church and entrepreneur Raj Alva, Whiskey JYPSI is premium small batch bourbon built on craft, character, and conviction. Nashville, TN. No apologies.
