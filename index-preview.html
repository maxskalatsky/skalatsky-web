Leadership

Max Skalatsky

Founder & Managing Partner

Max spent years building intelligence networks as an FBI Special Agent: recruiting, running, and managing human and physical assets across multiple continents. The skill set was simple: identify the right people, understand their motivations, and enable their performance.

He never stopped doing that. He just changed who the agents are.

Following the FBI, Max built a career spanning strategy, operations, and technology, working across consulting, product, and executive leadership, and culminating as a Global Vice President of Product Marketing at one of the world's premier enterprise technology companies.

These experiences gave him something most people building AI agents never develop: a ground-level understanding of the hurdles large organizations face designing and executing marketing and technology strategy. The cycles, the handoffs, the gaps between great ideas and live results. He envisioned and built S&A to eliminate these costs.

Max holds an MBA from Duke University's Fuqua School of Business and a BS in Management Information Systems from the University of Maryland, College Park.
