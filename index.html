Max Skalatsky

Founder & Managing Partner

Max Skalatsky spent years building intelligence networks as an FBI Special Agent: recruiting, running, and managing human and physical assets across multiple continents. The skill set was simple: identify the right people, understand their motivations, and get them to perform.

He never stopped doing that. He just changed who the agents are.

After the FBI, Max built a career spanning strategy, operations, and technology, working across consulting, product, and executive leadership, and culminating in three years as a Global VP at one of the world's premier enterprise technology companies.

That experience gave him something most people building AI agents have never had: a ground-level understanding of what marketing strategy and execution actually costs inside large organizations — the cycles, the handoffs, the gap between a great idea and a live result — and he designed and built S&A to eliminate it. The A-Team is how he brings that knowledge to life.

Max holds a Master of Business Administration (MBA) from Duke University's Fuqua School of Business and a Bachelor of Science (BS) in Management Information Systems from the University of Maryland, College Park.
